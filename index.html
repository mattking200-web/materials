People make waste every day. Waste is stuff we throw away. We can make less waste at school or home. This helps our Earth stay clean!

Kinds of Waste We Make

We make different kinds of waste. Like paper from drawing, plastic from toys or bags, food scraps from lunch, and old clothes that are too small.

Actions to Make Less Waste

Reduce: Use less stuff. Like using both sides of paper to draw. This is important because it saves trees!

Reuse: Use things again. Like using a cloth bag instead of new plastic ones. This helps because less plastic goes to the trash!

Recycle: Turn old things into new ones. Like putting paper in the recycle bin. This is good because it makes new paper without cutting more trees!

Compost: Put food scraps in a special bin to make soil. This is important because it helps plants grow and keeps trash smaller!

Our Plan to Minimize Waste

Let's make a plan! At school, we can use less paper by drawing small pictures. At home, we can reuse jars for storing toys. Tell your family why: It keeps our world clean for animals and us!
